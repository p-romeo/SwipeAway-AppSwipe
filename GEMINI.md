# SwipeAway Branding Identity Checklist

Avoid any modification that deviates from standard SwipeAway design guidelines. Below is the quick reference guidelines:

- **Brand Name**: SwipeAway
- **Primary Color Accents**: Deep Navy `#07112F`, Electric Blue `#1B5CFF`, Bright Cyan `#18D9E6`, Hot Pink `#FF0A7A`.
- **Swipe Overlay Left**: `"KEEP ⚡"` with Electric Blue background.
- **Swipe Overlay Right**: `"SWIPE AWAY 🗑️"` with Hot Pink background.
- **Tone & Typography**: Playful, modern, sans-serif fonts with soft shadows and generous negative spaces. Simple labels: Keep, Swipe Away, Review, Undo, Continue, Done.
